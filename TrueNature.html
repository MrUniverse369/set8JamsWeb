What To Expect

A Narrative driven platformer that does not compromise on the gameplay/mechanics, you will face diverse enemies that will force you to be creative in the way you tackle conflict. The difficulty of your journey will be dependent on the choices that you make and the path you choose to follow. True Nature does not seek to reinvent the platformer genre, instead the idea is to create something new from the solid foundations that already exist in the platformer genre.

Current State and Updates

Currently True Nature is development paused whilst i work on Harveys Nine Lives.

Most of the main character's player controller is complete although this is always subject to change since its early development. I have also added two environments to explore and I am going to continue building the gameplay around these two environments before adding more environments. A Lot more work still needs to go into the lore and building the story and the world. This will become more of a focus as I begin to feel the gameplay is engaging/fun to a point where the story compliments the game instead of the game depending on the story to keep players engaged as we all know not everyone plays for the story. My current focus right is on stabilizing the current build so that I can have it available for early access for you to play and also get you involved in the development process by offering your feedback
